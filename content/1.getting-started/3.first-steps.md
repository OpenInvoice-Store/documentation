## Creating Your Account

### 1. Sign Up

1. Navigate to your OpenInvoice instance (typically `http://localhost:3000` in development)
2. Click **Sign Up** or navigate to `/auth/sign-up`
3. Enter your email address and create a password
4. Verify your email if required by your Clerk configuration

### 2. Create or Join a Workspace

After signing up, you'll be prompted to:

- **Create a new workspace** - For personal use or to start a new organization
- **Join an existing workspace** - If you've been invited by a team member

Workspaces allow you to organize invoices, customers, and team members. You can switch between workspaces at any time.

## Initial Setup

### 1. Configure Company Branding

Set up your company information:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Branding**
2. Upload your company logo
3. Set your brand colors (primary and secondary)
4. Add company contact information:
   - Company name
   - Address
   - Phone number
   - Email
   - Website
5. Set your default currency
6. Customize footer text (optional)

This information will appear on all your invoices.

### 2. Set Up Payment Processing (Optional)

To accept online payments:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Payments**
2. Connect your Stripe account (see [Stripe Connect Setup](/developer-guide/integrations/stripe-connect))
3. Configure payment settings

### 3. Configure Tax Settings (Optional)

Set up tax calculation:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Tax** (if available)
2. Choose between:
   - **TaxJar** - Automatic tax calculation (requires API key)
   - **Custom Tax Profiles** - Manual tax configuration
3. Set your default tax profile

## Create Your First Invoice

Now you're ready to create your first invoice:

1. Go to **Invoices** → **New Invoice**
2. Select a customer (or create one on the fly)
3. Add line items (products or services)
4. Set invoice date and due date
5. Review totals and tax
6. Click **Save** or **Save & Send**

For detailed instructions, see [Creating Invoices](/user-guide/invoices/creating-invoices).

## Quick Tips

- **Use the Command Palette**: Press `Cmd+K` (Mac) or `Ctrl+K` (Windows/Linux) to quickly navigate and perform actions
- **Keyboard Shortcuts**: Most pages have keyboard shortcuts - look for hints in the UI
- **Dashboard Overview**: Check the **Overview** page for quick insights into your business
- **Kanban Board**: Use the Kanban board for visual invoice workflow management
