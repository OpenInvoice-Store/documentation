## Main Navigation

The OpenInvoice dashboard is organized into several main sections:

### Dashboard

The **Overview** page provides a high-level view of your business:

- **Key Metrics** - Total revenue, outstanding invoices, paid invoices
- **Revenue Charts** - Visual representation of your income over time
- **Invoice Status Distribution** - Pie chart showing invoice statuses
- **Sales Performance** - Bar charts and trends

### Invoicing

The invoicing section includes:

- **Invoices** - View, create, and manage all invoices
- **Recurring Invoices** - Set up and manage recurring invoice templates
- **Customers** - Manage your customer database
- **Products** - Maintain your product/service catalog

### Kanban

Visual invoice workflow management:

- Drag and drop invoices between status columns
- Quick status updates
- Visual overview of your invoice pipeline

### Reports

Advanced reporting features:

- **Custom Reports** - Build custom reports
- **Customer Lifetime Value** - Analyze customer value
- **Forecasting** - Financial forecasting tools

### Settings

Configure your account and workspace:

- **General Settings** - Account preferences
- **Branding** - Company branding and logo
- **Templates** - Invoice template management
- **Payments** - Payment processing configuration
- **Tax** - Tax settings and profiles
- **Notifications** - Email and notification preferences
- **AI Configuration** - AI features setup (optional)

## Key Interface Elements

### Command Palette

Press `Cmd+K` (Mac) or `Ctrl+K` (Windows/Linux) to open the command palette. This allows you to:

- Quickly navigate to any page
- Search for invoices, customers, or products
- Perform common actions

### Workspace Switcher

Located in the top navigation bar, allows you to:

- Switch between workspaces
- Create new workspaces
- Manage workspace settings

### User Menu

Access your profile, settings, and sign out from the user menu in the top right.

## Keyboard Shortcuts

OpenInvoice includes keyboard shortcuts for common actions:

- `d` + `d` - Go to Dashboard
- `i` + `i` - Go to Invoices
- `r` + `r` - Go to Recurring Invoices
- `c` + `c` - Go to Customers
- `p` + `p` - Go to Products
- `Cmd/Ctrl + K` - Open command palette

## Invoice Details Page

When viewing an invoice, you'll see a left sidebar with sections:

- **Details** - Invoice information and line items
- **Payments** - Payment history and add payments
- **Emails** - Email history and send invoice
- **Notes** - Internal notes and comments
- **Reminders** - Payment reminder history

## Best Practices

1. **Organize with Workspaces** - Use separate workspaces for different businesses or clients
2. **Use the Product Catalog** - Save time by adding products to your catalog
3. **Set Up Recurring Invoices** - Automate regular billing
4. **Configure Branding** - Make your invoices professional with custom branding
5. **Track Payments** - Record all payments to keep accurate financial records
