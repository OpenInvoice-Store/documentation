## Customer List View

The customer list provides:

- **Search** - Search by name, email, or company
- **Filters** - Filter by tags, status, or custom fields
- **Sorting** - Sort by name, total revenue, invoice count
- **Bulk Actions** - Select multiple customers for batch operations

## Customer Analytics

View customer insights:

### Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)

See the total value each customer has brought:
- Total revenue from customer
- Average invoice value
- Number of invoices
- Payment history

### Customer Activity

Track customer engagement:
- Last invoice date
- Last payment date
- Invoice frequency
- Payment patterns

## Customer Segmentation

Organize customers into groups:

### By Revenue
- High-value customers
- Medium-value customers
- Low-value customers

### By Activity
- Active customers
- Inactive customers
- New customers

### By Payment Behavior
- On-time payers
- Late payers
- Partial payers

## Customer Communication

### Email History

View all emails sent to a customer:
1. Open the customer
2. Go to **Emails** tab (if available)
3. View email history
4. See engagement metrics

### Payment Reminders

Send payment reminders to specific customers:
1. Filter invoices by customer
2. Select overdue invoices
3. Send bulk reminders

## Customer Payment Methods

Manage customer payment preferences:

### Saved Payment Methods

If Stripe is connected:
- View saved payment methods
- Set default payment method
- Remove payment methods
- View payment method details

### Payment Preferences

Set customer preferences:
- Preferred payment method
- Payment terms
- Billing frequency

## Customer Notes

Add and manage customer notes:

### Internal Notes

- Only visible to your team
- Track important information
- Add reminders
- Document conversations

### Customer Notes

- Visible on invoices
- Add terms or special instructions
- Include contract details

## Import/Export Customers

### Export Customers

1. Go to **Customers** page
2. Click **Export**
3. Choose format (CSV, Excel)
4. Download file

### Import Customers

1. Go to **Customers** page
2. Click **Import**
3. Upload CSV or Excel file
4. Map columns
5. Import

## Customer Tags

Organize customers with tags:

- **Create Tags** - Define custom tags
- **Assign Tags** - Tag customers
- **Filter by Tags** - Find customers by tags
- **Bulk Tag** - Tag multiple customers

## Customer Reports

Generate customer-specific reports:

- **Customer Revenue Report** - Revenue by customer
- **Customer Invoice Report** - All invoices for a customer
- **Customer Payment Report** - Payment history
- **Customer Lifetime Value** - CLV analysis

## Best Practices

1. **Regular Updates** - Keep customer information current
2. **Segment Customers** - Organize by value or behavior
3. **Track Communication** - Document all interactions
4. **Monitor Activity** - Identify inactive customers
5. **Build Relationships** - Use notes and personalization

## Customer Retention

### Identify At-Risk Customers

- Low activity
- Late payments
- Declining revenue
- No recent invoices

### Retention Strategies

- Send personalized messages
- Offer discounts or incentives
- Follow up on overdue invoices
- Request feedback
