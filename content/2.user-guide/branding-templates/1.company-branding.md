## Overview

Company branding allows you to customize how your company appears on invoices, making them professional and consistent with your brand identity.

## Accessing Branding Settings

1. Go to **Settings** â†’ **Branding**
2. View and edit branding options

## Branding Elements

### Company Logo

Upload your company logo:

1. Click **Upload Logo**
2. Select image file
3. Crop/resize if needed
4. Save

**Logo Guidelines**:
- Recommended size: 200x200px
- Supported formats: PNG, JPG, WebP
- Transparent background recommended
- Max file size: 5MB

### Brand Colors

Set your brand colors:

- **Primary Color** - Main brand color (hex code)
- **Secondary Color** - Accent color (hex code)

Colors are used for:
- Headers and accents
- Buttons and links
- Invoice styling
- Email templates

### Font Family

Choose font for invoices:

- **Default** - System default
- **Serif** - Traditional serif fonts
- **Sans-serif** - Modern sans-serif fonts
- **Monospace** - Fixed-width fonts

### Company Information

Add your company details:

- **Company Name** - Your business name
- **Address** - Street address
- **City** - City
- **State/Province** - State or province
- **Postal Code** - ZIP or postal code
- **Country** - Country
- **Phone** - Contact phone number
- **Email** - Contact email
- **Website** - Company website URL

### Footer Text

Customize invoice footer:

- **Footer Text** - Custom text displayed at bottom
- **Terms & Conditions** - Link to terms page
- **Privacy Policy** - Link to privacy policy

### Default Currency

Set default currency for your organization:

- Select from currency list
- Used for new invoices
- Can be overridden per invoice

## Preview

Preview your branding:

1. Make changes
2. Click **Preview**
3. See how invoice looks
4. Adjust as needed

## Applying Branding

Branding is automatically applied to:

- **Invoices** - All invoices use branding
- **PDFs** - Generated PDFs include branding
- **Emails** - Email templates use branding
- **Payment Pages** - Payment forms use branding

## Branding Best Practices

1. **Consistent Colors** - Use brand colors consistently
2. **High-Quality Logo** - Use high-resolution logo
3. **Complete Information** - Fill in all company details
4. **Professional Footer** - Include relevant links
5. **Test Appearance** - Preview before sending

## Branding Tips

### Logo Tips

- Use transparent PNG for best results
- Ensure logo is readable at small sizes
- Keep logo simple and clear
- Test on different backgrounds

### Color Tips

- Use colors that match your brand
- Ensure good contrast for readability
- Test colors on invoices
- Consider accessibility

### Font Tips

- Choose readable fonts
- Consider professional appearance
- Test font rendering
- Ensure consistency
