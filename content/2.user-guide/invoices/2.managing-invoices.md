## Invoice List View

The invoice list provides a comprehensive view of all your invoices with:

- **Search** - Search by invoice number, customer name, or amount
- **Filters** - Filter by status, date range, customer, and more
- **Sorting** - Sort by date, amount, status, or customer
- **Bulk Actions** - Select multiple invoices for batch operations

## Filtering Invoices

Use filters to find specific invoices:

### By Status
- Draft
- Sent
- Paid
- Partially Paid
- Overdue
- Cancelled

### By Date Range
- Today
- This Week
- This Month
- This Quarter
- This Year
- Custom Range

### By Customer
Filter invoices by specific customers

### By Amount
Filter by invoice amount ranges

## Invoice Actions

### View Invoice Details

Click on any invoice to view:
- Complete invoice details
- Payment history
- Email history
- Notes and comments
- Reminder history

### Edit Invoice

1. Open the invoice
2. Click **Edit**
3. Make changes
4. Save

> **Note**: Some fields may be locked for paid invoices.

### Duplicate Invoice

Create a copy of an existing invoice:

1. Open the invoice
2. Click **Duplicate**
3. Edit as needed
4. Save as new invoice

### Delete Invoice

Delete invoices that haven't been paid:

1. Open the invoice
2. Click **Delete**
3. Confirm deletion

> **Warning**: Deleted invoices cannot be recovered.

## Bulk Operations

Select multiple invoices to:

- **Export** - Export selected invoices to CSV or Excel
- **Change Status** - Update status for multiple invoices
- **Send Emails** - Send invoices to customers
- **Delete** - Remove multiple invoices

## Invoice Details Page

The invoice details page includes several sections:

### Details Tab
- Invoice information
- Line items
- Totals and tax breakdown
- Customer and company information

### Payments Tab
- Payment history
- Add new payment
- Payment plans and installments
- Payment receipts

### Emails Tab
- Email history
- Send invoice via email
- Email tracking (opens, clicks, delivery status)

### Notes Tab
- Internal notes
- Customer notes
- Activity log

### Reminders Tab
- Payment reminder history
- Send manual reminder
- Automated reminder settings

## Exporting Invoices

Export invoices in various formats:

1. Select invoices (or use "Select All")
2. Click **Export**
3. Choose format:
   - **CSV** - For spreadsheet applications
   - **Excel** - Excel-compatible format
   - **PDF** - Individual PDF files

## Invoice Numbering

Invoice numbers are auto-generated but can be customized:

- **Format**: Configurable in settings
- **Sequential**: Automatically increments
- **Custom**: Can be manually set per invoice

## Best Practices

1. **Regular Review** - Review invoices regularly to track status
2. **Follow Up** - Use reminders for overdue invoices
3. **Organize** - Use filters and tags to organize invoices
4. **Backup** - Export invoices regularly for backup
5. **Track Payments** - Record all payments promptly
